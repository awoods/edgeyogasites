Angel Stone CPT

Founder, http://www.eshebodycenter.com

Angel Stone is the Chief Executive Officer of Eshe Body Center. Her training philosophy stems from a lifetime of fitness and adventure. At the age of 6, she won her first foot race. That sprint across the playground started her running career and culminated in her first marathon (not a sprint). Motivated by that accomplishment, Angel went on to win a slot on the rowing team at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. After graduation, she became an officer in the United States Army. It was there that her love for fitness reached its height. The military gave her a greater appreciation for life and taught her to live every day to the fullest.

A nationally certified personal trainer and group exercise instructor, Angel maintains a rigorous fitness regimen. In addition, Angel is an active member of the dance community. She has performed internationally and also danced with a popular hip hop company. If you look closely, Angel can still be seen dancing and teaching hip hop classes throughout Metro DC.

LaRue is a certified personal trainer with over 14 years training experience and 20+ years in competitive sports.  He has trained a wide variety of people, including general fitness clients and athletes – adults and children alike.  LaRue was selected to the first National Board of Fitness Examiners, Board of Examiners in 2005; one of only two personal trainers in the country selected for this honor.

Health and Fitness Writer

LaRue has been writing for nine years and has had his health and fitness articles published in national, regional, and local publications. He serves as a Sports Science Writer for Tennis Life Magazine.  He has also written a book for improving the strength and fitness for racquet athletes, and is working on another on exercise for the overweight.

Fitness Programs and Consulting

Through his company, LaRue offers a myriad of health and fitness programs. He has successfully worked with clients looking to lose body fat, reduce inches, and live a healthier lifestyle. He also offers fitness and exercise instruction to beginners, couples, and small groups.  LaRue has developed sports conditioning programs and trained athletes from beginners to elite and nationally ranked athletes participating in a number of sports including tennis, baseball, basketball, hockey, softball, swimming, running, and football.  His Tennis Fitness Program has been used to train several junior tennis players who are ranked nationally by the United States Tennis Association (USTA), and adults and junior players looking to improve their fitness levels and reduce the risk of injury.  LaRue is himself a competitive tennis player, and in 2004, 2005, and 2006 was listed as a Top Ten ranked player in both Virginia, and in the Mid-Atlantic by the USTA.

Ryan Jankowitz

Owner, Dynamic Performance LLC

www.thedynamicperformance.com

I have been training in the Northern Virginia/DC Metro area since 2005 and am presently certified as a Personal Trainer through the American Council on Exercise (ACE) as well as a Level I-Kettlebell Instructor through Kettlebell Concepts (KBC).  My passion for health and fitness started while attending Virginia Tech where I began competing in all natural bodybuilding shows and received my Bachelor of Science in Human Nutrition, Foods and Exercise in 2004.  In 2008, I formed my own personal training company, Dynamic Performance LLC.

In addition to personal training, I also served as the assistant cross country coach at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology in the fall of 2007.  It was a very successful season as both the boys and girls teams became Liberty District champions.  In addition, the boys went on to become Northern Region and Virginia State champions.  My study and practice of Kung Fu since 2006 has shown me that not one mode of exercise will achieve one's goals.  All components of health and fitness; i.e. weight training, aerobic exercise, flexibility training, and healthy eating habits must be met in order to achieve one's goals and truly be fit.
