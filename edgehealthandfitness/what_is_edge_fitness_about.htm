What is Edge Fitness about?

Edge Fitness & our trainers are dedicated to helping our clients meet their weight loss & conditioning goals in a safe & effective fashion.  We form partnerships with only the most qualified, experienced & well educated conditioning specialists in Northern VA. Our trainers are all full time fitness professionals who have a vested stake in setting a professional & safe work environment.

How does the studio work?

Edge Fitness is a completely private training studio. It is facility in which only trainers & their respective clients work together. There are no members. Therefore we have no monthly or annual membership fees or dues !

If you are interested in personal training please go to our pricing and policies and fill out our questionnaire on our PRICING & POLICIES !
