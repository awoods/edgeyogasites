Drop-in Policies

"Drop-Ins" are welcomed & encouraged at each class!!!

At Edge Yoga we want to expose our students to as many different styles & teaching philosophies as possible.

There is no fixed class schedule & passes are accepted at every class & are good 6 months from the date of purchase!

Mats are available through the studio, but students are encouraged to bring a towel and bottled water to each class

Pricing & Studio Services

(1) Drop-in & Multi-class passes
As a drop in the fee is $16.00 per class or $13.00 for students with a valid local school ID. Class passes are available but can currently only be purchased at the studio via check or cash. A five class pass is available at $75.00 ($15.00 a class); ten class pass is available for $140.00 ($14.00 a class); & a twenty class pass for $260.00 ($13.00 a class).

Please note all pass sales are non transferable & good for ONLY 6 MONTHS from the date of purchase.

(2) Monthly Membership
For a 3 month commitment at $125 per month a student can attend an unlimited number of weekly classes. After the initial three month commitment the contract reverts to a month to month basis with a 30 day cancelation notice requirement.

(3) 1 on 1 In-Home Yoga Instruction
One on One in home sessions are also available through Edge Yoga at a cost of $150.00 / hr but are left to the discretion of the individual instructors.

(4) 1 on 1 Yoga Instruction
One hour One on One sessions are available at a cost of $80.00 / per 60 minute session through the studio during non reserved times.
