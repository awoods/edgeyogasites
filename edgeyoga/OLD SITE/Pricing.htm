Drop-in Policies
(1) Drop-in & Multi-class passes

As a drop in the fee is $15.00 per class or $12.00 for students with a valid local school ID. Class passes are available but can currently only be purchased at the studio via check or cash. A five class pass is available at $70.00 ($14.00 a class); ten class pass is available for $135.00 ($13.50 a class); & a twenty class pass for $250.00 ($12.50 a class) .

Please note all pass sales are non transferable & good for ONLY 6 MONTHS from the date of purchase.

(2) 1 on 1 Yoga Instruction

One hour One on One sessions are available at a cost of $75.00 / per 60 minute session through the studio during non reserved times.

(3) 1 on 1 In-Home Yoga Instruction

One on One in home sessions are also available through Edge Yoga at a cost of $100.00 / hr but are left to the discretion of the individual instructors.

(4) Off Site Teaching

Off site teaching is available at a cost of $135.00 to $155.00 / 75 minute class, but is left to the discretion of both the teacher & Edge Yoga !
